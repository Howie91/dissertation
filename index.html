Job Description

You have already been introduced to your team. This is our highest performering bankers and you have the pleasure of managing them for the next 12 months.

Greenfield Capital Management's goal is to maximise long-term profits and thus we are prepared for short-term losses.

The bankers' job is fairly straightforward. Each month they receive $10m in fresh cash from our investors and they then have to decide whether to invest the $10m in either;

1. A risky stock portfolio in their respective market.
2. Risk-free government bonds.

If invested in stocks, the value of the investment will either increase or decrease. Luckily, our analytics department has developed an astonishing algorithm that calculates the potential outcomes and the likelihood that each outcome will occur.

If invested in bonds, the investment will increase by the promised amount of $0.1m with 100% certainty.

To keep the bankers satisfied we pay them a bonus every month. Your job is simple, at the end of each month you will allocate a total of $100,000 in bonuses between the three.

Please note: The information provided by the analytics department is the only information the bankers have available, thus, you will have access to the exact same information as they do.
